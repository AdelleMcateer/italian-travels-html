Naples - the home of pizza

Naples or Napoli is the regional capital of Campania and the third-largest city of Italy after Rome and Milan. In 2017, around 967,069 people lived within the city's administrative limits while its province-level municipality has a population of 3,115,320 residents.

Its continuously built-up metropolitan area (that stretches beyond the boundaries of the Metropolitan City of Naples) is the second or third largest metropolitan area in Italy and one of the most densely populated cities in Europe.

Things to do in Naples

  • Walk the famously narrow streets at sunset.
  • Eat pizza - Naples is traditionally credited as the home of pizza.
  • Take a trip to Posillipo hill for a stunning view of the Bay of Naples.
  • Climb Mount Vesuvius for panoramic views of the Bay of Naples..
  • Visit Pompeii.

It is a very unique city and is a good starting point if you wish to travel to Sorrento and on through the Amalfi Coast. It is also close to Pompeii & Vesuvius.
